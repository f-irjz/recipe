Classic Lasagna

A rich and comforting Italian classic layered with savory sauce, tender pasta, and creamy cheese—perfect for a cozy homemade meal.

Watch the full recipe video

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or plant-based alternative
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic, onion, olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, Italian herbs

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and cook the lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil, then add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce, season with herbs, salt, and pepper, and simmer gently.
  4. Layer sauce, noodles, and cheeses in a baking dish until full.
  5. Bake uncovered for 35–40 minutes until bubbling and golden.
  6. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
