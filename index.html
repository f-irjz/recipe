Classic Lasagna

A timeless Italian comfort dish layered with rich tomato sauce, creamy cheese, and perfectly cooked pasta. Simple, hearty, and endlessly satisfying.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or plant-based alternative
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic, onion, olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, Italian herbs

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil, then add ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce, season with salt, pepper, and herbs, and simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  4. Layer sauce, noodles, and cheeses in a baking dish, repeating until full.
  5. Finish with mozzarella and parmesan on top, then bake for 35–40 minutes.
  6. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
